There are 30 major league baseball teams. Each of these teams has a owner and president of baseball operations. These people run their teams and make decisions about the future of their team. The comisioner of Major League Baseball is Ron Manfred. He makes the decisions about the league.

Rob Manfred has been the comisioner of baseball since 2015. Since then he has been controversial and not like by a majority of baseball fans.
